About the College Introduction & Principal's Message

Dr. K.K. Mandal College, Jagdishpur, established in 1979, is an affiliated rural college spread over 5.1 acres of land in Bhojpur district, Bihar. The college is affiliated to Veer Kunwar Singh University (VKSU), Ara, Bihar and was formally affiliated in 1982. The college is registered under 2(f) and 12B of UGC and has been accredited by NAAC.

Situated in the heart of Bihar, the college offers Intermediate, Undergraduate and select Post-Graduate programmes in Arts, Science and Commerce streams. The college also provides vocational courses like BCA and BBA to meet the needs of today's industry.

Our vision is to provide quality education to students from all sections of society, especially those from rural, SC/ST, and OBC backgrounds, helping them develop critical thinking, personality, and moral values to become responsible citizens.

The college campus is Wi-Fi enabled and houses a well-equipped library, science laboratories, a computer lab, sports facilities, girls' hostel, and a canteen.
